YSFlight is a free* flight simulator that places the user in control.

* – Free is really free. We'll never charge you for anything. We promise.

Basic avionics, forgiving flight models, and uncomplicated weapons systems make YSFlight easy to learn, while a vibrant modding and online flight community draws new fans in and holds veterans’ attention.

Try this amazing free flight simulation today and you too will find that though this simulator may not have the world’s greatest graphics, it is like nothing else the world has ever seen - and it’s all FREE.

Sandbox-Like Freedom

YSFlight provides a very wide range of opportunities and scenarios many other sims don’t get to offer. Hop into your favorite modern fighter jet and wage battle against enemy aircraft using fast maneuvering missiles or use half-ton bombs to take out massive ground facilities while flying Santa's sleigh. Go back into the first era of combat flight and fly vintage aircraft into battle. Step into the cockpit and fly passengers across the Hawaiian Islands for a spring break vacation to Hilo or use the search and rescue helicopter to bring some unlucky spring breakers home after their boat catches fire off the Honolulu coast. Fly the fastest and most heavily armed planes you want in YSFlight. The only limits are your imagination and your add-on collection.

Some of the possibilities…

  • Air Combat

    Air combat is a big part of YSFlight, both offline and online. You can create your own dogfights, or fly in one of the predesigned missions included with the installation.

  • Vehicles

    YSFlight is much more than a flight simulator. There is also a wide array of ground vehicles, from military humvees to super cars to ATVs and boats. If you can dream it, you can drive it.

  • Missions

    With YSFlight, there are many default missions for you to play, these include simple air to ground missions, and the introduction of new ground to air mission you can be one of the turret operators who must defend the airport from attacking air forces, or you can challenge your skills as a commercial pilot, and land in some of the harshest environments known to YSFlight.

  • Weather

    One of the lesser known features of YSFlight is the various weather customizations it offers. You can change the time from day to night for an added challenge, or set the cloud ceilings to be very low. You can also change the amount of fog to practice flying under hazardous conditions.

Play With Friends, Join the Community

With multiplayer, you can join your friends on servers to do just about anything you can’t always do alone. Join together in teams and dogfight "East v. West" battles in modern or cold war era aircraft, or show off your skills doing aerobatics for your friends. YSFlight free flight simulation provides the amazing opportunity to host online events such as virtual airshows, squadron competitions, or even races! YSFlight’s extensive online community has been around for nearly a decade, and host all kinds of amazing events. The community is constantly creating addons for everyone, and always giving advice to newcomers.

  • Vibrant Community

    Here at YSFlight headquarters, we have a vibrant community with many virtual groups, events, and other activities.

  • Multiplayer

    Fly with your friends online in YSFlight's multiplayer mode. Everything available in single player so also available in multiplayer, so check out the server list and double your fun.

  • Civilian Flight

    If you aren't a fan of air combat, you can also do free flight and pilot a jetliner across an ocean. There are multiple virtual airlines looking for new pilots to join, so help them increase their revenue.

  • Aerobatics

    One of the most fun aspects of the game is aerobatic flying, where you perform death-defying stunts and pollute the air with airshow smoke. You can perform maneuvers in a formation, or as a single ship demonstration. Keep practicing your skills and get the chance to perform at airshows!

A Classic Game Given New Life

Under continual development by Soji Yamakawa (since 1999), YSFlight free flight simulator provides a growing list of features, with a substantial set of maps and aircraft in the initial download. The true strength of the game, however, is the modding community. YSFHQ houses an extensive collection of new maps, aircraft, weapons skins, and tools, while the international community provides thousands of additional mods to try. If you can’t find what you really want, make it yourself! With relatively simple modding tools, almost anyone can produce basic repaints, while greater efforts can produce high quality jet fighters, transformable mechs, or a man on a bicycle.

Scenery

Unlike many flight simulators, YSFlight has thousands of user-customizable maps which provide players with a place to fly over, drive on, targets to attack and runways to land on. Many maps have been modeled on real-world locations such as Hawaii, Arizona, or the UK. There are ground attack maps, civilian airports, airshow locations, and much more. For a lot more maps, check out YSFHQ's scenery forum.

  • Vietnam

    By Major

    A nicely well made map by Major that takes place during the Vietnam War. A must have for Vietnam Era enthusiasts.

  • 171st Sapian

    By CVW-171

    The 171st Sapian map is the home for the CVW-171 Squadron. Complete with a navy fleet and high detail airports with air defenses, its not to be taken lightly.

  • UK+

    By Gunny

    The UK+ map by Gunny is a scaled down replica of the United Kingdom with airports in Northern Ireland, Wales, England and Scotland. Best of all, for its size - it surprisingly doesn't lag.

  • Helgoland

    By Waspe

    A very small map consisting of two islands and highly detailed. Perfect size to fly a single propeller airplane and marvel at the detail.

  • Arizona

    By Stykkisholmur

    Airzona by Stykk is a very high detailed representation of Arizona in the United States.

  • Edwards AFB

    By Taskforce58

    The Edwards AFB map is a replica of the real Edwards AFB with extreme detail, its a very cool map to fly on and simulate Edwards AFB operations.

  • St. Maarten

    By Taiwan

    St. Maarten by Taiwan is a highly detailed map of the famous St. Maarten airport. The airport is famous for its low approach over a beach just before the airport which many people visit.

  • Hawaii [Griffon]

    By Griffon

    Hawaii by Griffon is a rework of the original Hawaii map included with the game, its huge detail and awesome night lights make it well worth a download.

  • SoCal

    By PatrickN

    This map is quite expansive and includes airports such as LAX, Van Nuys, Bob Hope, Compton, and Long Beach airports. Also included is the Hollywood sign and the downtown LA area, including City Hall.
